My Favourite Book

My favourite book is The Master and Margarita written by Mikhail Bulgakov. In Russian the title of the book is Мастер и Маргарита. The novel is a famous work of Russian literature that mixes fantasy, satire and romance.

The story takes place in Moscow and tells about the mysterious visit of the devil and his strange companions. At the same time it tells the love story between the Master and Margarita and a story about Pontius Pilate. You can read more about the book on this page.
